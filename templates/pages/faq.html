Unlabel Creator Rates

How are Creator rates negotiated? Does Unlabel act as an agent and take a percentage? What is the average rate for brand collaborations?

We will be emailing you product options so you can collaborate on paid projects with any brand on-demand. Your rates are calculated based on your Unlabel Creator Likeness Score.

What is the Unlabel Creator Likeness Score?

Your Creator Likeness Score helps determine how much you get paid per product collaboration, which is calculated by our algorithm.

I am new to Unlabel and haven’t done a product review yet, so do I have an Unlabel Creator Likeness Score?

No. All Creators who are new to Unlabel will have a default rate of $50 per post. Your rate will increase from this baseline according to your Unlabel Creator Likeness Score, and will build over time.

Content Creation Process

How long do I have to respond to product selection options?

You have 24 hours from the time we email you to respond with your product selections.

How long will it take to receive product?

Delivery times will vary from brand to brand, but we will send you tracking information once it becomes available.

How much time do I have to create content for each project?

You have 5 days from the time the package is received (based on the tracking information) to create and email us the content you’ve produced

Under special circumstances we may be able to extend this deadline, but advance notice must be given.

Content Promotion Guidelines

What are my options for promoting Unlabel and the products I work with?

Your Creator Profile on unlabel.us acts as a portfolio for your branded content. You choose how you want to drive traffic to each product on Unlabel, whether it Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Snapchat etc…so you have full control over your owned social networks, and can stay as authentic as possible. However, we require that one of the social channels you post to is Instagram, as the platform has the highest levels of engagement. Even an Instagram story is adequate to show proof of promotion. Ultimately your project rates are determined by your success in promoting these products, so choose the platforms that work best for you!

Are there any guidelines on how we should promote a post on a particular (online) channel?

Yes, each guideline is listed below:

  • Direct traffic to the item you featured by linking the exact item on the Unlabel Consumer Marketplace.

  • The item link in the Unlabel Consumer Marketplace must be used as the click-through link for the post.

    • Snapchat

    General Questions

    What is the Consumer Marketplace?

    The Consumer Marketplace is where your branded content is posted. You can engagement from your social channels to your Creator Profile on the Consumer Marketplace so your audience can see all the collaboration you’ve done and purchase the items they see you post.

    What kinds of brands can I find on Unlabel?

    Unlabel works with a wide variety of brands ranging from avant-garde, to minimal, to street style. We try to curate our marketplace to feature unique products you’ll love, and hope Unlabel helps you discover your new favorite brands. We are always open to hearing suggestions about brands you want to see on the platform —just send them to creators@unlabel.us. At this time our focus is on curating emerging and independent brands.
